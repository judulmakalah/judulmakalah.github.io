7. b inggris masing masing 2 contoh1.present tense (2 contoh)--2.pas comtinous--3.pas tense--


1. Simple Present Tense

Kalimat positif: S + V1, jika subjeknya she, he, dan it maka V1 + s/es

She goes to the school

They write the book


2. Past Continous Tense

Kalimat positif: S + was/were + V1 ing


She was going to the school

They were writing the book


3. Simple Past Tense

Kalimat positif : S + V2

She went to the school

They wrote the book
